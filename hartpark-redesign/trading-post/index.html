The Trading Post Gift Shop

William S. Hart Park and Museum

Our Collection

The Gift Store is stocked with a wide variety of unique merchandise including Western themed books, music, movies, jewelry, purses, home decorations, fine artwork, and classic toys, alongside a selection of cold drinks and snacks.

Of special interest to historic film enthusiasts is our collection of Bill Hart memorabilia. We carry DVD copies of more than 15 of his classic silent films, souvenir mugs, custom apparel, and original prints.

Featured Editions

We feature high-quality printings of classic historical volumes, including the excellent 2013 reproduction of William S. Hart's autobiography, "My Life East & West" (originally published in 1929).

A limited edition of the definitive softcover volume "The Complete Films of William S. Hart" is also available, providing rare production photographs and historical overviews of every single project in his historic career.

Plan Your Visit

Operating Hours: Saturdays 10am to 2pm, weather permitting. Also open during special Park Events and by appointment for private tours or school field trips.

Location: Conveniently located near the main Park entrance, directly nestled between the historic Ranch House and Hart Hall, right next to the animal pens.

Supporting the Park: Every dollar spent at the Trading Post goes directly to the Friends of Hart Park, funding ongoing care for our park animals, structural upgrades, and public community events.
