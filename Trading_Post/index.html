THE TRADING POST GIFT SHOP

William S. Hart Park and Museum


The Gift Store is stocked with a wide variety of merchandise including Western theme books, music, movies, jewelry, purses, home decorations, art work, toys and other items including a limited amount of snacks and cold drinks.

Of special interest to many Park visitors is the Bill Hart memorabilia collection that includes DVD copies of more than 15 of his silent films, souvenir mugs, pens and pencils, hats, framed photos, original copies of books written by Hart, and the 2013 excellent quality reprinting of his autobiography, “My Life East & West” ©1929. A limited edition of the soft cover book “The Complete Films of William S. Hart” is also available.

Shop in our Store!

The Friends of Hart Park “Trading Post“ Gift Store stocks a DVD collection of Wm. S. Hart’s silent films, including his last and most famous feature “Tumbleweeds”. The limited edition soft cover book, “The Complete Films of William S. Hart” provides photographs and an overview of each of his films. Individual DVDs and a special 5-pack collection are available.

Visitors to the Gift Store will find that we provide free Park information sheets and a Park map. We look forward to having you drop by the Gift Store!

Operating hours: Weekends 11:00am to 3:30pm. Also open during special Park Events and by appointment through the Museum for tours and school fieldtrips. Contact the Museum staff at www.hartmuseum.org to arrange a visit to the Store.

Location: The Gift Store is conveniently located near the main Park entrance between the Ranch House and Hart Hall and next to the animal pens.


The Friends of Hart Park Gift Store operates to provide funds for use throughout Hart Park. Your support of the Gift Store aids in the care of Park animals, maintenance, upgrades, and sponsorship of events.
