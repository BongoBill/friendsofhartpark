Adjusting your screen resolution
  
 
  • Windows 95/98/2000 - Operating System
    		•   
     

    1. Click your "Start" button
    2. Go to "Settings" > "Control Panel"
    3. Click on the icon labeled "Display"
    4. Click on the "Settings" tab
    5. Next move the slider control in the "Screen Area" section
        to the right/left until the numbers read "1024 by 768"
    6. Click the "Apply" button

    NOTE: You may be asked to restart your computer.
