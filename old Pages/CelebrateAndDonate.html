Celebrating the Holidays and William S. Hart's Birthday!
   
 

 

Dear Friends of Hart Park and Museum Supporter,

This year the Friends of Hart Park and Museum will regrettably not be hosting the annual celebration of William S. Hart’s birthday with our holiday event, Cowboys and Carols. As many of you know, Hart Park and Museum is currently undergoing renovations on various buildings including the chauffeur’s quarters, horse barn and log cabin building. Hart Museum is in the process of having its tile roof replaced as well as work completed on doors, windows and balconies in order to better protect the historic mansion and artifacts housed inside.

Unfortunately due to the ongoing work on the Hart Museum, the building will not be available for our annual holiday celebration. The Hart Museum will reopen for public tours on Wednesday, December 22, 2010. The Museum will be decorated in its traditional holiday splendor making this season a wonderful time to bring family and friends.

As with all of our events, monies are raised to support Hart Park and Museum buildings, animals and infrastructure. This year we are hoping to complete a supply of emergency preparedness materials that would cover 30 people for 3 days. Hart Park is an emergency shelter location by supplying food, water and other basic necessities to the local community in case of a disaster.

May we suggest that when you consider your holiday giving you consider a donation to the Friends of Hart Park. Donations may be made by clicking the donate button here:

We thank you for your continued support.
