THE TRADING POST GIFT SHOP

             William S. Hart Park and Museum            

Native American and contemporary Western jewelry is now featured at the Store and there is an excellent selection in various price ranges.

For the recent Cowboy Festival we stocked up on many new DVD titles -- Roy Rogers, John Wayne, Hopalong Cassidy, Johnny Mack Brown, Harry Carey, Annie Oakley, Lone Ranger, Hoot Gibson, and our featured Western actor...William S. Hart. A new Hart film, White Oak, has just arrived, and that brings our selection of Hart films to 16 titles. Special box collections of films are available at great prices and will provide hours of viewing fun!

Our Store is open on the weekends from 11am to 3:30pm and for special events in the Park. Please stop by for a visit, and we do appreciate your mentioning the Trading Post to your friends!

See you at the Park!


Becki Basham
Gift Store Manager
