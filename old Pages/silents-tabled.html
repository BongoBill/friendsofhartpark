What?

Howdy Fans of Hart Flickers!

Mosey on over to Hart Park for this year's Silents Under the Stars!

This year, we'll be doing something different, and something we think y'all will enjoy.  While the main Mansion up the hill is getting some needed refurbishment, we'll be holding "Silents" closer to ground level.  We'll have the usual, lip-smackin' good dinner served in Hart Hall, followed by desserts provided by the Friends of Hart Park volunteers.  Then we'll retire out back to watch the silent movie, under the stars, in the beautiful patio setting. 

The movie this year will be one of Hart's later films that marked the return of Bill's favorite horse Fritz to the screen.  Enjoy a movie practically dedicated to Fritz, just a few yards from Fritz's final resting place!  Also, back by popular demand, the movie will once again be accompanied by The Leasebreakers Jug Band! 

Prior to dinner and the movie, experience docent-led tours of Fritz's grave, Hart's dog graveyard, and best of all Hart's ranch house where he lived while the house up the hill was being built.  Hear stories of Bill's horses and dogs, and see artifacts from Hart's film career, along with a special display of his saddle collection.

Enjoy newly developed tours of some of the less-visited gems in Hart Park!  Enjoy viewing a rare Hart film!  All in all, enjoy a memorable "Silents Under The Stars" that is guaranteed to be different than any you've seen before!
