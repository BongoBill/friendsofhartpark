    What?

    Spanning 500 years in one day, William S. Hart Park presents ¡Viva Fiesta!, a celebration of the culture, music and food of Mexico. ¡Viva Fiesta! will be presented in two acts.

    ¡Viva Fiesta!, act one, is a "Thank You" to our Hispanic neighbors. Come to the park between 11AM and 4PM for free entertainment and fun for all in the spirit of "Mi casa es su casa!"

    Featured artists for Viva Fiesta!:

    • Benny and Rosa Martinez - Masters of the Vaquero Tradition in Trick Roping and featuring Napoleon, the wonder horse!
    • Mariachi Cobras de Jalisco (of San Fernando)
    • Ritual Azteca Neuhi Ollin
    • Ballet Folklorico (of Santa Clarita)
